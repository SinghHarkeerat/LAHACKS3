About Us

At SpeakForMe.AI, our mission is to bridge the communication gap for the deaf and hard of hearing community. Our innovative lip tracking technology provides live, accurate subtitles for real-time conversations—without the need for any audio. This empowers individuals to fully engage in conversations, presentations, and media, making accessibility more seamless and inclusive. Founded by Nripesh, Harkeerat, Ryan, and Muhaimin, we are a diverse team driven by a shared passion for technology and accessibility. Together, we’re committed to creating solutions that make a real difference in the lives of those who need them most.
